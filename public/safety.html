EchoStream – Child Safety Standards

EchoStream is committed to protecting children and maintaining a safe environment. We enforce a zero-tolerance policy toward any content involving child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE/CSAM).

1. Zero-Tolerance Policy

EchoStream strictly prohibits:

  • Any sexual content involving minors
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors
  • Requests for or distribution of CSAE content

2. In-App Reporting

Users can report content directly within the app using the Report option on posts or profiles. All reports are reviewed promptly, and violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent bans.

3. External Reporting

If a child is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services. Suspected CSAM may also be reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC):

https://www.missingkids.org/gethelpnow/cybertipline

4. Enforcement

EchoStream cooperates with law enforcement when required and may preserve relevant data to comply with legal obligations. Severe or repeated violations result in permanent account termination.
